THE STORY

Born in the heart of Sharjah in 2025, Nimir's Pizzeria exploded onto the scene. In just a short time, we've become a Muwaileh legend, fueled by a simple promise: Real NYC Slices. No gimmicks, just artisanal craft and the spirit of NYC in every bite.

48-HOUR PERFECTION

Our dough isn't rushed. We use a 48-hour cold fermentation process to develop that signature NYC-style airy, crispy crust. Hand-stretched, stone-baked, and served with pure passion.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

DO YOU DELIVER TO DUBAI?

We currently focus on providing the freshest experience within Sharjah (Muwaileh & surrounding areas) to ensure our crust stays perfectly crispy.

WHAT MAKES YOUR DOUGH SPECIAL?

It's the 48-hour cold fermentation. This breakdown process creates a dough that is not only more flavorful but much lighter and easier to digest.

ARE THERE VEGAN OPTIONS?

Yes! Our dough is vegan-friendly by default. Our Veggie pie can be made without cheese, and we're always experimenting with new plant-based recipes.
